ABOUT US

With the six to eight-years experiences in using voices, dubbing and translation sectors, creating the meaning, emotional and entertained products is our main motivation. It is not only to create effects, impressive sounds and wonderful images, but we also want our communication products containing emotion with people’s attractive voices.

OUR STORY

When we are in our mothers’ womb, we can hear our dad and mom’s voices. Sometimes we can hear the music. This form the brain, emotion and the behaviour. When we grow up, the images which are not familiar are not encoded through the eyes of children. Consequently, the words from our environment have formed the special connection whereas we do not understand this connection. Voices are the method transmitting from the past to now when we do not have written scripts, books and symbols.
Voices ,which are not sterile as written scripts and are not only for exchange information, affect the emotion and bring people together and release the inner hurts. With the six to eight year experiences in using voices, dubbing and translation sectors, creating the meaning, emotional and entertained products is our main motivation.
We have written our story about fantastic and meaning sound. We keep the Vietnamese purity and enhance the people’s strength which is their voices. We bring the meaningful messages to our customers by voices through the use of modern communication products.
